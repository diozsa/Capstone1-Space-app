Perseverance Rover

Perseverance is the latest rover deployed on Mars by NASA. It has 15 cameras on board dedicated to specific imaging tasks. The app randomly selects a sol (Mars day) from the total days the rover has been active and retrieves pictures from different cameras. These images have no description - some are more interesting than others.
Click the link again to refresh or click on an image to zoom in.
